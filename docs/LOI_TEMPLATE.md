## 2. What this letter is

I am participating in ProdScope's private design-partner program. During this
program I receive unlimited app-analysis runs on my production app at no
charge, in exchange for honest, structured feedback and permission to quote my
company on the ProdScope website (with my explicit approval before publication).

This letter records my intent to convert to a paid ProdScope subscription
once billing ships, subject to the product continuing to meet my needs.

## 3. Intent to convert

On the date ProdScope launches paid billing, I intend to subscribe at the
tier and price agreed below, **subject to the product continuing to meet my
needs at that time**.

- **Target monthly price:** `$[X]` per month
- **Maximum I would consider paying:** `$[Y]` per month
- **Contract length:** month-to-month, cancellable any time
- **Invoicing:** card or monthly invoice, whichever ProdScope supports at launch

If the launched price exceeds `$[Y]`, or the product has materially regressed
from what I evaluated during the design-partner program, I am not obligated
to subscribe.

## 4. What ProdScope commits to

- Keeping the design-partner program free for the duration of the private
  beta (no retroactive billing for pilot runs).
- Notifying me at least 14 days before pricing launches, with the final tier
  and price.
- Not making a binding commitment on my behalf based on this letter.
- Honoring a first-access invite and a founding-customer price locked in
  for 12 months once billing ships, if I convert within 30 days of launch.

## 5. What I commit to (non-binding)

- Running ProdScope on my production app at least once.
- Providing written feedback via the structured form ProdScope sends after
  each run (typically 5–10 minutes).
- A 20–30 minute call at the end of the program to review findings and
  pricing reaction.
- Approving or declining, in writing, any testimonial or case study
  ProdScope proposes to publish about my app — ProdScope will not publish
  without my approval.

## 6. Explicit non-obligations

This letter does **not**:

- Create a contract, purchase order, or subscription.
- Commit me or my company to pay any amount.
- Transfer any IP, data, or credentials beyond what is voluntarily shared
  during the pilot.
- Bind either party to exclusivity or non-compete terms.

Either party may exit the design-partner program at any time, for any reason,
by sending an email. There is no notice period and no penalty.
